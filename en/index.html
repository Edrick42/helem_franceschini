Who I am
Who I am

Attorney. CEO.
Speaker.

I'm an attorney and Executive Director of Franceschini Advogados Associados, a boutique strategic litigation firm based in Rio de Janeiro.

My purpose is clear: to protect business trajectories through applied legal strategy.

With 16 years of practice, I work in legal strategy for asset protection, Digital Law, LGPD (Brazilian General Data Protection Law) and legal technology. I preside over the Future of Law Committee at OAB-RJ (Rio de Janeiro Bar Association) and coordinate the Artificial Intelligence and Legal Practice program at ESA/OAB-RJ.

Speaker on Digital Law and legal technology. In 2026, I was part of the Brazilian delegation at the Deposit Ceremony in the Arctic World Archive in Longyearbyen, Norway.

Positioning

I'm not the lawyer who talks about technology.
I'm the entrepreneur who already operates with it.

When I step on a stage, the business owner doesn't see a specialist merely explaining AI. They see someone who thinks like them, understands business, risk and decision-making, and uses the law as an instrument to protect what they have built.

What I believe

Convictions that guide
every decision.

Technology is not the future of the law — it is already the present. Those still debating whether to adopt it have already fallen behind.

Business owners don't need to understand the law. They need someone who understands their business and uses the law to protect it.

Loyalty is not a differentiator. It is a foundation. Without it, no strategy works.

A misstep in the digital environment can destroy in days what took years to build.

Legal strategy is not reaction — it is anticipation.
